pkmns.com

pkmns.com is an online community where users can adopt, raise, and collect Pokemons. This project ran for 2 years with over two-million adopted pokemons, over $10,000 in revenue, and an vibrant community.

I was one of the co-founder leading the technical development, UX design, and managed a team of 7 individuals to support the growth of this website.
