pkmns.com

pkmns.com is an online community where users can adopt, raise, and collect Pokemons. This project ran for 2 years (2012 - 2014) with over two-million adopted pokemons, over $10,000 in revenue, and an vibrant community.

I was one of the co-founder leading the technical development, UX design, and managed a team of 7 individuals to support the growth of this website.

You can view an archive of the website here.

Evolution of Certs


Certs are the foundation of pkmns.com. They are the foundation for how users interact with their beloved adoptable Pokemons.

Over the years, we evolved our cert designs along side with the release of Pokemon Black and White. One of the main focus in the redesign was increasing contrast and legibility within the cert design. Another focus was incorporating the new artistic styles of Pokemon Black and White itself.

User Growth

Within our first year, we launched a monthly donator collectable program that involved special collectable pokemons being released once a month. These monthly donation pkmns helped support the growth of pkmns.com and faciliated really cool collectable trades within the larger community. We were also able launch a engagement campaign titled "Sawsbucks" that involved a brand new website redesign and community engagement games led by our community moderators.

The Site:

pkmns.com was initially release with the intention of being a niche community. The original concept was a spin off site for gaiaonline.com to complement a forum RPG. After gaining traction within other Pokemon communities and growing our user base, we pivoted from being a complementary site into a full fledge community. This involved launching new features, hiring new artist and developers, and developing long term visions for the website.
